I'm Jack Smith, a digital product designer and front-end developer based in Nottingham, England.

I mainly specialise in designing user interfaces for both the web and mobile applications. Crafting brilliant user experiences and stand-out products is what I'm truly passionate about, and there's nothing I enjoy more than achieving this through innovative design.

My other skill-set lies in front-end development. HTML, Sass, and interaction/animation based JavaScript.

My experience includes working for agencies, startups, and as a freelancer. Read on for more about how I work, and let me know if you're interested in working together.

Hiring and workflow details

What sort of projects are you after?

I normally favour working with early-stage startups on web and mobile apps. Fast paced prototyping, MVPs, etc, in particular. Also happy to help out agencies now and again.

What design software do you use?

Sketch, Chrome/Sublime, Photoshop, Origami, Invision.

Will you work on-site?

While I generally work remotely, I am available for meetings and some on-site contracts in Nottingham. May also be available for meetings or workshops in London. Remote means a lot of work around Slack, Trello, and Invision.

Are you looking for a full-time job?

Not right now.

What's your rate?

£350 ($500) a day.

Availability?

Available straight away (actively scoping new projects).
