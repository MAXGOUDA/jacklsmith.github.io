I'm Jack Smith, a digital product designer and front-end developer based in London, England.

I mainly specialise in designing user interfaces for both the web and mobile applications. Crafting brilliant user experiences and stand-out products is what I'm truly passionate about, and there's nothing I enjoy more than achieving this through innovative design.

My other skill-set lies in front-end development. I've been laying down scalable HTML, CSS, and interaction/animation based JavaScript for over 5 years.

My experience includes working for agencies, startups, and as a freelancer. As of May 2015, I have some availability upcoming. Read on for more about how I work, and let me know if you're interested in working together.
