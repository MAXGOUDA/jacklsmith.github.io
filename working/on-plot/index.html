Plot

Designing movie discovery for the iPhone.

View plotapp.io

Plot is an iPhone app for discovering, planning, and sharing the movies you watch.

A collaboration with Ben Howdle, I've been leading the design side of things, while Ben focuses on the iOS and Node.js development.

Frustrated by the hordes of bloated and poorly designed solutions out there, we wanted to build a simple and slick app for sharing and reviewing what you watch, collating the movies you want to see, and a base to explore numerous ideas around movie discovery.
